Measuring dominance in the Tour de France

To gauge the extent of the Tour de France winners dominance, one can analyse various statistics such as the number of their stage victories, the number of stages in which they wore the leader's jersey, and the margin the winner maintained over the second-place rider in the general classification. These metrics collectively reveal the level of dominance exhibited by the Tour de France champion.
